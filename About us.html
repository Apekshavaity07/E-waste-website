MISSION

An effort or campaign to address the growing problem of electronic waste, which is a major environmental and health concern. E-waste is any discarded electronic or electrical device, including computers, smartphones, televisions, refrigerators, and other electronic gadgets.To Tackle e-waste involves various activities aimed at reducing the amount of e-waste generated, promoting responsible disposal of electronic devices, and encouraging the recycling and reuse of electronic materials.


VISION

To create a world where electronic waste is managed in a responsible and sustainable manner.This vision includes Zero waste,Circular economy,Environmental protection, Social responsibility etc.a collaborative effort from governments, businesses, civil society, and individuals. By working together, we can create a world where electronic waste is managed in a way that benefits both people and the planet.


ACHIEVEMENTS

Achievements in the field of e-waste management in recent years. Some of these achievements include E-waste legislation,Public awareness,Extended producer responsibility,Increased recycling rates etc.However, there is still much work to be done to ensure that electronic waste is managed responsibly and sustainably, and to promote the transition to a circular economy.
